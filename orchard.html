Our Orchard

Newcastle Orchards sits in California's Sierra Foothills, where citrus has been cultivated for generations.

Our 4.6-acre orchard is home to approximately 400 Owari Satsuma mandarin trees.

These seedless mandarins are prized for their sweetness, easy-to-peel skin, and rich flavor.

The Seasonal Cycle

  • Spring: Blossoms emerge.
  • Summer: Fruit develops.
  • Autumn: Mandarins mature.
  • Winter: Harvest season.
